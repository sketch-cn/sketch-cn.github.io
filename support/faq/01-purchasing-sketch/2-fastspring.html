Business and Educational

Can I purchase multiple licenses for Sketch?

Yes, we offer business packs licenses, with discounts based on the number of licenses purchased. To make a purchase, visit our store.

Are there floating licenses?

No, they're to be exclusively used on up to as many seats as purchased, and just that. If you need to disable/enable a license, please contact our customer support.

I need to add an extra seat to my previously purchased pack, can I?

As of now, it's not possible, we're working on that. You can always purchase a second volume license pack of course.

I'm a student, do you provide any specific discount?

Yes, we provide a 50% discount for students, to be eligible for one, contact our customer support and provide the following:

  • School ID photo/scan
  • Enrolment proof valid for the current studying year.

As a teacher, am I eligible for an educational discount on volume licenses?

Yes, please contact our customer support and provide the following:

  • Staff ID photo/scan
  • Job description (what do you teach, age group, country)

Is there any missing feature when compared to the Mac App Store version?

There is no iCloud support in this version. iCloud support is exclusive to Applications sold and distributed exclusively on the Mac App Store.

Can I transfer my purchase from the Mac App Store?

Contact our customer support and we'll evaluate your case, and if we see fit, we'll definitely help.

Do you provide re-selling?

No. We do not allow or support any kind of reselling of Sketch.
