Reporting Bugs

I've found a bug in Sketch, how can I report it?

Sketch has a built-in tool to provide bug reports, crash reports, request features and give general feedback.

Go to the "help" menu and select "contact customer support".

Please provide as much info as possible, every detail counts. If possible, upload your working file to a service like dropbox (always .zip it first) and provide the link in the description, due to Sketch nature, most bugs are related to a specific element of your design and we need to inspect it in order to provide a fix.

All the information shared with us is 100% confidential and not stored.

Any other way to get in touch with your customer support team?

Yes, you can contact directly by email.

Alternatively, you can get in touch with us through twitter or facebook.
