In the Mac App Store

Where can I purchase Sketch?

Sketch is available for purchase on the Mac App Store. To purchase it, click here.

Can I install Sketch on multiple computers?

Yes, you can install Sketch on more than one computer, after purchase, just login to your Mac App Store account on each computer and download it.

I need an invoice, where can I request it?

Billing and Invoices are exclusively handled by the Mac App Store staff, in case you didn't receive an email with your invoice after 48h, contact their customer support. Same applies for refunds.

If you purcahsed Sketch via our own store you will have received an invoice as part of your purchase already.

Do you provide a trial?

Yes, we provide a 14 days trial that is available for download at our website.
