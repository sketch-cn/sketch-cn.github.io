From Sketch 2 to Sketch 3

Hello, i've purchased Sketch 2, am I eligible for a free upgrade to version 3?

Customers who purchased Sketch 2 after March, 01, 2014 are eligible for a free upgrade. To take advantage of this offer, please contact our customer support and provide:

  • Mac App Store receipt confirming your purchase

We cannot provide you with a promo code to download Sketch 3 on the Mac App Store, as those are limited in quantity. However, we can provide you a download link and a license key for our version outside the Mac App Store.

Does this non-Mac App Store version work as the Mac App Store one?

No, it doesn't provide iCloud support, other than that, it gets the same updates, at the same time.

Can I use this serial on more than one computer?

No, it only provides a license to one machine.

I've purchased an educational license, am I eligible too?

If you've made the purchase after March, 01, 2014, yes. Please provide the invoice of your purchase and we'll set up a new serial key for you.

I've purchased a business pack, am I eligible too

If you've made the purchase after March, 01, 2014, yes. Please provide the invoice of your purchase and we'll set up a new serial key for you.

Is there any upgrade price for business pack licenses?

Yes, please get in touch with customer support.
